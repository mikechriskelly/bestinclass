Why We Succeed

What Makes Us Different

Our Proprietary Material

Our two-part approach to learning and teaching lays the foundation for skill development. Our proprietary materials build a solid foundation of knowledge and promote analytical and critical thinking skills. Our teachers invest time in correcting and reviewing the homework with students.

Technology

By utilizing BC software, you will be able to create a database to track potential students and manage your current roster. Best in Class' proprietary curricula are the most important resources that we provide; they are what set us apart. By utilizing our printing software, we give you full access to the most current curricula, assessments, answer keys, and training programs. This software helps to minimize cost and reduce overhead.

Marketing and PR

In preparation for the grand opening of your center, our public relations firm will work with you to learn more about your story, your background, and your center so we can assist in building the Best in Class brand in your community before construction has even begun!

Support Team

From operations, finance, IT, HR, and marketing, you will have an entire team behind you!
