Reach Further

Our programs move students of all ages and abilities toward the top of their class. Empower your child with the gift of a lifetime.

Enrichment

Small group classes engage students in critical thinking and application of skills and knowledge. Enrichment classes can be used for supplemental or remedial purposes, or for gifted students who need extra challenges.

Private Tutoring

With one-on-one attention from our highly educated tutors and learning activities created specifically for each student, your child is sure to make tremendous progress. Tutoring is available for a wide variety of subjects.

Gifted Test Prep

Scoring well on gifted tests like the ITBS and CogAT can set your child up for a trajectory of success. This group class program thoroughly prepares students for the test-specific content they will encounter as well as proven test-taking strategies.

Our Approach to Education

Best In Class Education is not just our name, but our motto as well. We believe that individual needs require individual attention, which is why we provide customizable material and tailored teaching to meet each student’s specific needs. Our programs are designed to develop students’ knowledge acquisition, critical thinking abilities, and application of skills. We also monitor each student’s progress every step of the way using daily homework exercises and weekly tests to ensure that they are on track and that we are doing everything we can to improve their learning.

Our goal is to motivate and encourage students to learn the basic principles of mathematics and English in a rewarding and enjoyable way. Your children will gain a sense of confidence and excitement towards learning unlike any they have experienced before. Get started today and give your children the gift of a lifetime.
