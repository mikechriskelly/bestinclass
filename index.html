Reach Further

Our programs move students of all ages and abilities toward the top of their class.

Enrichment

Small group classes catered to students’ individual strengths and areas for improvement in math and English. Program builds a foundation of skills and critical thinking to supplement school curriculum.

Private Tutoring

Experienced, private tutors work with students one-on-one – or two students can share a session. Students receive tailored assistance to build study skills, confidence and independence. Ideal for high school and middle school students seeking to achieve subject mastery.

Gifted Test Prep

Courses for kindergarten through fourth grade students, providing test-taking strategy and test preparation for gifted program entrance exams.

Our Approach to Education

Best In Class Education is not just our name, but our motto as well. We believe that individual needs require individual attention, which is why we provide customizable material and tailored teaching to meet each student’s specific needs.

Our programs are designed to develop students’ knowledge acquisition, critical thinking abilities, and application of skills. We also monitor each student’s progress every step of the way using daily homework exercises and weekly tests to ensure that they are on track.

Our goal is to motivate and encourage students to learn the basic principles of mathematics and English in a rewarding and enjoyable way. Your children will gain a sense of confidence and excitement towards learning. Get started today and give your children the gift of a lifetime.
