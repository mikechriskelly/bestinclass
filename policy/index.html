Privacy Policy

We Respect the privacy of every individual who visits this site.

This Privacy Policy explains how bestinclasseducation.com ("Website") collects and otherwise processes personally identifiable information ("Personal Information").

Best in Class Education Center ("Best in Class") respects and understands the importance of privacy and security for our online customers. Any information collected is used primarily by Best in Class and its affiliate companies and our Best in Class franchisees to contact consumers for marketing and operational purposes. Persons who supply us with their telephone numbers or email addresses on-line may receive telephone or email contact from us or from our Best in Class franchisees with information regarding special promotions, new products, and services or upcoming events.

You may choose to submit Personal Information (such as your name, address, email address and telephone number) to us at several different points on the Website. From time to time, we may request Personal Information from you on the Website in order to deliver requested materials to you, respond to your questions, or deliver a product or service. We will not share this information in ways different from what is disclosed in this statement.

Collecting and Using Personal Information

When you visit and navigate the Website and when you communicate with us via the Website, we will not collect Personal Information about you unless you provide us that information voluntarily. Any non-public Personal Information that you may provide via the Website will be used solely for the purpose stated on the page where it is collected. (In some cases, and in all cases where required by law or regulation, you will be able to update the information that you provide to us either by sending us an email or, where you have established personal profiles with us, by updating your profile online. Please refer to the specific pages where data is collected for more information.) Best in Class will not sell, license, transmit or disclose this information outside of Best in Class and its affiliated companies unless: (a) expressly authorized by you; (b) necessary to enable Best in Class contractors or agents to perform certain functions for us; or (c) required or permitted by law. In all cases, we will disclose the information consistent with applicable laws and regulations and we will require the recipient to protect the information and use it only for the purpose it was provided.

Your Information and Opting Out

If you do not wish to receive periodic mailings or emails, or telephone contact concerning our Website, our services, reminders and/or upcoming events, you may opt-out of receiving such marketing or promotional communications by sending us a written request to the address listed below or by following the opt-out instructions in the email you receive from us. However, if you choose to opt-out of marketing or promotional communications, we reserve the right to contact you regarding your orders, technical support and any other matter that might affect our service to you, and/or your use of the Website, as applicable.

Your Email

We welcome your comments or questions about the Website and have provided email boxes for that purpose. We will share your comments and questions with our customer service representatives and those employees most capable of addressing your questions and concerns.

Please note your email, like most, if not all, non-encrypted Internet email communications, may be accessed and viewed by other Internet users, without your knowledge and permission, while in transit to us. For that reason, to protect your privacy, please do not use email to communicate information to us that you consider confidential. If you wish, you may contact us instead by telephone at the numbers provided at various locations on the Website.

Other Information and "Cookies"

You should also be aware that when you visit the Website, we collect certain information that does not identify you personally, but provides us with "usage data," such as the number of visitors we receive or what pages are visited most often. This data helps us to analyze and improve the usefulness of the information we provide on the Website.

Like most commercial website owners, we may use what is known as "cookie" technology. A "cookie" is an element of data that a website can send to your browser when you link to that website. It is not a computer program and has no ability to read data residing on your computer or instruct it to perform any step or function. By assigning a unique data element to each visitor, the website is able to recognize repeat users, track usage patterns and better serve you when you return to that site. The cookie does not extract other personal information about you, such as your name or address.

We cannot, however, be responsible for the privacy practices used by other website owners or the content or accuracy of those other websites. Links to various non-Best in Class websites do not constitute or imply endorsement by Best in Class of these websites, any products or services described on these sites, or of any other material contained in them.
