Teaching Assistants

Our teaching assistant program is an excellent opportunity for juniors and seniors in high school, as well as for college students that have excelled academically, to learn about the rewards of teaching and making a difference in the lives of younger students.

Teaching Assistants handle a variety of responsibilities, including grading student work, maintaining progress records, as well as administrative duties such as answering phones and assisting in tutoring elementary-age students.
