Careers

Passion for education and satisfaction in helping students succeed runs deep in our DNA here at Best in Class Education Center, and we take adding new members to our team very seriously. If you believe you possess knowledge that could be passed on as a gift to others and you’re looking for a fast-paced, thriving environment where new ideas are welcome, where teamwork and collaboration occur daily and would like to work in place where ensuring student success is not an option, but a requirement, then you could become a valued member of the Best in Class family.

Available Positions

Private Tutors and Instructors

We’re looking for candidates who have at least a four-year degree and two tears of teaching experience. Available positions for subjects and grade level vary by location. Pay is DOE and competitive, and we offer 401(k) and medical benefits for those who are eligible.

If you have browsed the different programs we offer and are interested in teaching any of them, please email careers@bestinclasseducation.com to inquire about any Washington location(s), or visit your nearest center’s page for the main contact: http://mikechriskelly.github.io/bestinclass/locations/

Teaching Assistant

Our teaching assistant program is an excellent opportunity for juniors and seniors in high school, as well as for college students that have excelled academically, to learn about the rewards of teaching and making a difference in the lives of younger students.

Teaching Assistants handle a variety of responsibilities, including grading student work, maintaining progress records, as well as administrative duties such as answering phones and assisting in tutoring elementary-age students.

*Teaching Assistants must be at least 16 years of age and be able to demonstrate advanced knowledge of math, proper grammar, spelling and English usage. Pay varies by location.

If you are interested in becoming a Teaching Assistant, please email careers@bestinclasseducation.com to inquire about any Washington locations, or visit your nearest center’s page for the main contact: http://mikechriskelly.github.io/bestinclass/locations/

Volunteer Program

The Best in Class volunteer program is geared toward providing high school students the opportunity to gain valuable work experience and leadership skills while sharing their knowledge with younger students. High school students who are entering 10th grade (or beyond) are eligible for the program and will be responsible for assisting younger students with various classroom activities, helping to grade student work, act as additional chaperones for field trips and support their assigned classroom teacher as needed.

Students who complete a minimum of 40 volunteer hours will receive a certificate of completion, and detailed recap of work performed for volunteer hours and a reference letter from his or her direct Best in Class supervisor.

Interested students should click here (hyperlink to application form) to complete the volunteer application form, and then email the completed form along with an essay describing how they feel they can benefit younger Best in Class students to stephaniez@bestinclasseducation.com
