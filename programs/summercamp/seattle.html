Join Summer Camp 2014

Best in Class Education Center brings you our Summer Camp. This is a great opportunity for children entering preschool through 9th grade to stay sharp, learn and have fun during summer time. Our program integrates MATH, ENGLISH, GIFTED TEST PREP, ARTS & CRAFTS, GAMES, and ACTIVITIES.

We give students a chance to learn and have fun at the same time. Students cooperate and learn collectively during our group activities which include math exercises, math word problems, gifted test preparation, logic exercises, reading comprehension, grammar, vocabulary, creative writing, reading, games, and much more. Students practice the lessons with the supervision and guidance of our helpful instructors. Children have never had so much fun before!

In addition to our educational material, we are proud to offer fun and exciting activities for our students as part of our Summer Camp. They offer students a chance to explore a variety of interests and help keep summer exciting. In the afternoons our students will enjoy the following activities: arts and crafts, nutrition and fitness, world knowledge, animal knowledge, and team and character building.

About Our Summer Camp Activities

Animal Knowledge

Best in Class students will journey into the world of animals during this activity. Students will learn about the incredible creatures of this planet, and spend some time researching an animal of their choice.

Nutrition and Fitness

The Best in Class Nutrition and Fitness activity is the perfect way for kids to stay active over the summer. Not only will students get exercise, but they will also learn about making healthy food choices. Students will learn about how different foods affect the body and what important nutrients our bodies need to help us stay healthy.

World Knowledge

The world is becoming a smaller place and our advanced technology is bringing people together like never before. At Best in Class, we value the importance of learning about other regions of the world, their histories, their customs, and their cultures. Students will journey through time and foreign lands to learn fun and important facts about other parts of our world.

Arts and Crafts

The Best in Class arts and crafts activity offers a fun and creative way to spend the summer. It is the perfect opportunity for your children to be creative, build artistic skills, and craft beautiful pieces of art.

Team and Character Building Activities

Learning to work together as a team is one of the most valuable skills for students to learn. Teamwork is vital for success throughout education and future goals. At Best in Class, we make learning these skills fun by incorporating interactive games and activities.
