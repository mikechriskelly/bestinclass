Have fun. Stay sharp.

School’s out for the summer, but the learning doesn’t stop! Best in Class centers offer a variety of summer camp programs to engage children in learning and staying sharp over the summer while having fun.

Our summer program combines various areas of focus from Best in Class and our partners' programs. Summer camp activities include:

  • Math
  • English
  • Health & Nutrition
  • Arts & Crafts
  • Games
  • Outdoor Activities
  • Drama & Theatre
