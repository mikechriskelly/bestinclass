The fun starts but the learning don't stop.

School’s out for the summer! But the learning doesn’t stop. Best in Class centers offer a variety of summer camp programs to engage children in learning and stay sharp over the summer while having fun.

Activities incorporate different areas of focus and each of centers partners with local activity centers as well so that students have the opportunity to enjoy fun, summer outings. Summer camp activities include:

  • Math
  • English
  • Health & Nutrition
  • Arts & Crafts
  • Games
  • Outdoor Activities
  • And more
