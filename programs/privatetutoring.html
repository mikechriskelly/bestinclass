Areas of Focus

  • Improve knowledge and performance in current course material
  • Build study skills, confidence and independence
  • Identify teacher expectations and prepare for upcoming classes
  • Tutoring available in all core academic areas at all levels (Includes subjects under the umbrella of math, English, science and technology)
  • Sessions can be tailored to test preparation (SAT, AP, TOEFL, ISEE, SSAT,ITBS, CogAT) or essay writing and college application assistance as well
  • Instructors qualified to tutor student in honors and AP subjects

Tutoring Sessions

Tutoring sessions are one hour long and provide students one-on-one dedicated time with the instructor. Students have the option of sharing their session with one other student if the area of focus is the same.

Whether a student is struggling with a particular subject or exceling and looking to move to the head of their class, tutoring sessions are uniquely tailored based on individual needs.

Instructors work with students to improve in areas of need while simultaneously teaching them new study and problem solving skills to enable them to think more independently when faced with academic challenges.
