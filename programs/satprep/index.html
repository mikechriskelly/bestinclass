SAT Test Prep

Hit the books. Work that brain. Train hard for test day.

Students receive the test-taking skills, strategy, and insights required to score higher in the math, verbal and literary skills categories within the current SAT. Our focus is on reading comprehension, SAT-focused vocabulary building, sentence structure, grammar, the formation of paragraphs and essay composition, as well as analytical dissection of challenging math problems.

SAT Prep Classes

These courses are designed to give students a competitive advantage over their fellow college applicants and a greater chance of acceptance to the college of their choice.

Test-taking strategies are introduced to students throughout the course, equipping them with test savvy such as the ability to identify clue, trigger, fluff or identifier words, determine when to skip a question or guess an answer, read efficiently under time constraints and much more.

With a variety of different activities – including review and class discussion of test techniques, practical concept applications and a series of practice tests (over 2,000 practice questions!) – Best in Class students become familiar with the different concepts they will be presented with and are able to think critically about how to effectively approach the questions.

Classes can be taken in a private lesson format one-on-one, two students per instructor, or in a group-lesson format with 6 to 10 students.

Areas of Focus

  • Test Taking Skills, Strategy & Tips
  • Mathematical, Verbal and Literary Skills Per SAT Requirements
  • Reading Comprehension, SAT Vocabulary, Sentence Completion & Grammar
  • Analysis & Breakdown of Math Problems
  • Paragraph & Essay Writing
