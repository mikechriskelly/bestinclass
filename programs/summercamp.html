Best in Class Summer Camp is the place for children to stay sharp, learn, and have fun for the summer. Our program integrates:

  • Math
  • English
  • Gifted Test Prep
  • Art & Crafts
  • Games
  • Outdoor Activities
  • And more

We give students a chance to learn and have fun at the same time. Students cooperate and learn collectively during our group activities which include math exercises, math word problems, gifted test preparation, logic exercises, reading comprehension, grammar, vocabulary, creative writing, reading, and much more.

Best in Class also partners up with several local activity centers to keep our summer camps exciting and to offer students a variety of options. Activities enable students to learn and have fun simultaneously. Activity options vary depending on location.
